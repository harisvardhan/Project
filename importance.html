The Importance of Water Protection

Why We Need to Protect Our Water

Water is essential for all forms of life, and yet it is often taken for granted. Here are some reasons why we need to protect our water:

  • Water is a finite resource. Only 2.5% of the Earth's water is fresh, and only a fraction of that is accessible to us.
  • Water is essential for agriculture. 70% of the world's freshwater is used for agriculture, and this percentage is expected to increase as the global population grows.
  • Water is essential for industry. Many industries, such as manufacturing and energy production, rely heavily on water.
  • Water is essential for human health. Access to clean water is essential for preventing waterborne diseases and maintaining good hygiene.
